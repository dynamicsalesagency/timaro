FAQs

How long does a typical build take?

Most builds ship in 2‚Äì4 weeks depending on scope and content readiness. Retainers run month-to-month with weekly releases.

Do you work with existing themes?

Yes. I can optimize your current theme or create custom sections to avoid a full rebuild, while still improving speed and UX.

What tools do you use for analytics?

GA4, Shopify reports, and lightweight dashboards. The goal is clarity, not dashboard theater‚Äîso we track only what drives decisions.

Can you help improve my store's conversion rate?

Absolutely. I analyze your store's layout, funnel structure, and user experience to boost conversions through A/B testing, trust elements, and optimized call-to-actions.

Do you provide marketing or ad management services?

Yes. I manage Meta (Facebook/Instagram) and Google Ads campaigns, focusing on ROI-driven strategies and creative testing to grow your traffic and sales.

Can you migrate my existing store to Shopify?

Yes. I handle full migration from WooCommerce, Wix, or Squarespace, including products, customers, and SEO redirects to maintain your existing rankings.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes. Every project includes free post-launch support, plus optional maintenance and marketing retainers to ensure ongoing growth and improvements.

Will my Shopify store be mobile-friendly?

Definitely. Every design I create is 100% responsive and tested across multiple devices to ensure a seamless mobile shopping experience.

Can you improve my site speed and SEO performance?

Yes. I optimize image compression, Liquid code, and third-party apps to boost speed and implement on-page SEO best practices for higher organic visibility.

What industries do you work with?

I've built and optimized stores in fashion, beauty, fitness, tech, home decor, and pet industries ‚Äî but I'm open to any niche with a strong product and growth vision.

Do you handle Shopify Plus projects?

Yes. I design and optimize Shopify Plus stores with advanced automation, checkout customization, and performance-driven storefronts.

Can you integrate third-party apps or custom features?

Absolutely. I connect Shopify with CRMs, fulfillment tools, and marketing apps‚Äîor build lightweight custom apps when needed.

How do you price your services?

Pricing depends on project scope. Store builds start with a flat-rate quote, while optimization or marketing retainers are billed monthly with clear deliverables.

Can you write product descriptions or create content?

Yes. I offer conversion-oriented copywriting and AI-assisted product content that matches your brand tone and improves search visibility.

Do you provide branding or logo design?

Basic brand and logo design can be included in Shopify setup packages, ensuring visual consistency across your store and marketing channels.

Will my store be optimized for Google Shopping and Meta Ads?

Yes. I structure product feeds, meta fields, and tracking pixels for smooth integration with Google Merchant Center and Meta Ads Manager.

Can you set up email or SMS marketing?

Yes. I integrate Klaviyo or Shopify Email with automated flows like welcome, abandoned cart, post-purchase, and win-back campaigns.

Do you offer training after project completion?

Yes. I provide video tutorials or a live walkthrough showing you how to manage your products, orders, and marketing dashboards with confidence.
